The electric car challenge

Norway has the higest number of electric cars in the world and there are more electric cars sold than diesel and pretrol cars combined.

The sucess of changing from fosil fuel to clean electricity has created challenges to our electric grid.

Energy consumption does have very high peaks during the day - prices vary according to this consumption profile.

Additional capacity needs to be provided to cope with these peaks.

On special occasions (end of the FA final) very high spikes are experienced.

Renewable energy is generated independent from the consumption profile (e.g. no solar power when the FA final finishes)

Smart grids need balancing to ensure stability.

Why Norway

Norway has vasts amounts of clean hydropower energy. One third of Europes clean, green energy is stored in the Norwegian mountains.
Norway als has the most electric cars in the world.
These two factors makes Norway the best place on the planet to create solutions that combine electric carrs and the use of clean electricity.
